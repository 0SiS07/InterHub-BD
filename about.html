The Intern HUB BD

Intern Hub BD is an interdisciplinary advanced-learning program at the Virginia Tech Transportation Institute (VTTI) that accelerates practical skill development in students through internships with high-tech employers in the transportation industry.

Intern HUB BD Goals

Our goal for our students

Speed up the development of practical skills required for the modern tech industry by using a practical and engaging approach

Our goal for our Industry Partners

Developing a sustainable and top-notch pool of skilled tech professionals for fast forwarding tech industry.

Program Leads

Program Director: Mr. Jassim

Mr. Jassim coordinates multiple experiential learning programs at VTTI and serves as program director for Intern Hub BD. His focus is ensuring that students that engage with VTTI gain valuable professional experience as they assist with our primary mission of saving lives. Mr. Jassim also serves as Associate Professor within the Department of Biomedical Engineering and Mechanics at Virginia Tech, where he oversees the research activities of nearly a dozen undergraduate and graduate students.
