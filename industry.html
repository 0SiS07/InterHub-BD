The Intern HUB BD

Intern Hub BD is an interdisciplinary advanced-learning program at the Virginia Tech Transportation Institute (VTTI) that accelerates practical skill development in students through internships with high-tech employers in the transportation industry.

The Program

The Intern Hub BD is a program that helps you gain the necessary skills to thrive in today's highly competitive job market and align your college education with your career goals. With The Intern Hub BD, you can apply the technical skills you learn in school to exciting projects, while also developing professional skills and building connections with industry partners in the high-tech field.

The Students

The Intern Hub BD aims to connect Industry Partners with talented students from diverse backgrounds. We encourage Industry Partners to offer internships to students, fostering a strong professional relationship. By the end of the internship, students will be highly skilled and suitable for future positions within the partner's organization. Building long-term relationships with The Intern Hub BD program ensures a continuous flow of talented individuals into partner organizations.
